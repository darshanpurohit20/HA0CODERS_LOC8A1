## üéØ Swipe Interface Mastery

### Gestures Guide
- **Swipe Right (Approve)**: Drag card 150px+ to the right OR swipe with velocity >500px/s
- **Swipe Left (Reject)**: Drag card 150px+ to the left OR swipe with velocity >500px/s  
- **Swipe Up (Skip)**: Drag card 150px+ upward to defer decision
- **Hover**: Card scales slightly (1.02x) to show it's interactive
- **Return**: If you don't swipe far enough, card springs back to center

### Pro Tips
- **Speed Swipe**: Flick cards quickly for rapid decisions
- **Careful Review**: Hover to read AI reasoning before swiping
- **Skip Smartly**: Use swipe-up when you need more information
- **Progress Tracking**: Watch the counter at top right
- **Undo**: Navigate to Approved Leads to review decisions

## ü§ñ Understanding AI Scores

### Vector Score (Blue)
- Measures semantic similarity between companies
- Higher = better product/market fit
- Based on: industry, products, target markets, company attributes
- Sweet spot: 80%+

### Intent Score (Purple)
- Captures buying signals and active interest
- Higher = more likely to engage
- Based on: platform activity, budget approval, procurement signals
- Sweet spot: 85%+

### Trade Momentum Index (Orange)
- Historical pattern matching
- Higher = similar to your successful deals
- Based on: deal velocity, market expansion, supply chain patterns
- Sweet spot: 75%+

### Match Percentage (Combined)
- Weighted average of all three scores
- 90-100%: Excellent match (top priority)
- 80-89%: Very good match (worth pursuing)
- 70-79%: Good match (review carefully)
- 60-69%: Marginal match (skip unless special criteria)

- Check AI toggle is ON (green)
- Verify lead is in approved status
- Allow 1-2 minutes for initial response
- Manual takeover if urgent
